What will you do without design?

When

It has been many years since the birth of design. I've been there almost since the beginning. Design is integral to our lives and when done well can improve, inspire and illuminate our existence.

How

As a designer I use all my senses to create a design. Most people think the most important sense to a designer is vision, but I've found that listening to users has proven to be the most valuable sense for arriving at a solution that satisfies.

Why

Designers solve problems. I like to invent little products. When walking around life I like to think of things that will make life easier. From space planning to tool creation design is at the root of an item that "just works".
